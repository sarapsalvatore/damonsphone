Rain hit the pavement in waves as you walked home alone.
Streetlights flickered. The city was almost empty.

Then you saw it.

An iPhone lying in the middle of the sidewalk.
Screen cracked slightly. Still glowing.

You picked it up.

The wallpaper made your heart stop for a second — a photo of Ian Somerhalder and Nina Dobrev laughing together.

For a moment you just stared at it.

No missed calls.
No notifications.
Nothing.

Only the lockscreen.

You tried to put the phone back down…
but something in your head kept whispering the same numbers over and over again.

000000.

You frowned.

Why would that even be the password?

Slowly, you typed it in.

The screen froze for a second.

Then—

Unlocked.

Your heart dropped.

Apps. Messages. Photos. Voice memos.
Everything was there.

Like someone wanted you to see it.
